Atomic Spectroscopy Simulation

This application is used in the chemistry laboratory as a teaching tool. During lab the students often move the spectroscopes and get them out of focus. Students do not know what they should be seeing and therefore they may collect the wrong data. This application helps students visualize the spectrum of Helium and Hydrogen and prevents students from collecting the wrong data.

Lockers Database

Database system that helps the CHemistry Department manage their lockers. The System assigns lockers to students, assign Teaching Assistants to rooms, provides information about locker combinations and contact information. The application was developed using HTML, CSS, Java Script, Ajax, PHP, and MySQL. The application is only available on the University of Colorado intra-net.
